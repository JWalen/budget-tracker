# Future Ideas

## iPhone App with Local Network Sync
- Native iOS app for quick data entry on the go
- When the phone joins the same network as the main app, automatically resync data
- Offline-first: entries are stored locally on the phone until sync occurs

## Ollama AI Assistant
- Local AI assistant powered by Ollama for budget insights and suggestions
- Spending analysis — "Where am I overspending this month?"
- Bill optimization — suggest which pay period to assign bills to based on due dates and amounts
- Budget recommendations — identify categories where limits could be adjusted
- Anomaly detection — flag unusual transactions or spending patterns
- Natural language queries — "How much did I spend on food last month?"
- Runs entirely locally via Ollama API — no data leaves the machine
